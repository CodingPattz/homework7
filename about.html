About Me



Miami based Graphic Designer originally from Caracas, Venezuela.

I like the creative process that involves the development of the corporate identity of companies that are about to start on the market, o require a new image to raise the impact they have on society. To me the Corporate Identity is a fundamental part of a company; is the tool that allows the association of an image to the service you can be paying.

Also I believe that the editorial design has an enormous potential and have to be exploited, so I tend to do jobs where it is evident the harmony of the layout of text and images, while respecting the client’s criteria.

Internet is a great platform, that’s why I gave listing advisory and I manage company accounts on social networks, with the aim of opening a solid space to any company or individual on the Internet, where the design and good taste are the common denominator.
